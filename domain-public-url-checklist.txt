Memorable public domain — registrar & branding checklist
==========================================================

1) Candidate stems (brainstorm — search .com availability for each you like)
   Brand: serenityrentals, serenitygulf, serenitygulfstays, gulfserenity,
          serenitypcb, serenityemerald
   Place: pcbdestinstays, emeralddirectstays, gulfcoastbyowner, twogulfhomes
   Direct: bookgulfdirect, gulfdirectstays, ownerdirectgulf

2) Phone-test finalists (pick 2–3, say aloud: "visit ______ dot com")
   - serenitygulf
   - serenitypcb
   - gulfdirectstays

3) Availability (you must run this at your registrar — e.g. Cloudflare, Namecheap, Google Domains)
   - Search each finalist + .com
   - Prefer no hyphen in the primary domain
   - If .com is taken, try small variants or secure .com + redirect from .vacations later

4) After purchase — single source of truth in code
   - Edit config.js: SITE_BASE_URL = 'https://YourDomain.com' (no trailing slash)
   - Reload the site; canonical, og:url, and JSON-LD url update automatically via app.js

5) Align email & social (manual reservation at provider)
   Suggested email prefixes on your domain: hello@, stays@, info@
   Social handles (check Instagram/Facebook/TikTok): e.g. @SerenityRentalsPCB,
   @SerenityGulfStays — use the same name everywhere when possible.
