Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Serenity Rentals ("we", "us") operates stayatflorida.com to list owner-managed Florida Gulf Coast vacation rentals. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Contact form. When you submit the contact or booking-inquiry form, we collect the email address, phone number (if provided), and message you enter. This information is delivered to us as an email through our form-handling provider.
  • Basic browser data. Like most websites, our host may record non-identifying request information (IP, user agent, referring page) for security and operational reasons.
  • Availability data. Availability shown on property pages is merged from the property owner’s platform calendars (Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com) and is stored as static files on this site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your booking inquiry or question.
  • To confirm pricing, availability, and payment details with you directly.
  • To keep the site running reliably and safely.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your information. We use the following third parties only to operate the site:

  • Web3Forms — delivers contact-form submissions to our inbox.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the static site.
  • OpenStreetMap — provides the map tiles shown on property pages.

These services process data only as needed to deliver their service.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not set advertising cookies and does not include third-party analytics by default. If that changes in the future, this page will be updated.

Your choices

You can contact us anytime at FloridaVacationRental2020@gmail.com to ask what information we have about you from prior inquiries, or to request its deletion.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.
